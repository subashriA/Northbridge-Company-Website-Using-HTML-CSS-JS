About Northbridge

Fourteen years of turning strategy into shipped products

We're a tight-knit team of strategists, designers, and engineers based in Boston, working with clients across North America and Europe.

Our Story

Founded by builders, not just consultants

Northbridge Consulting started in 2012 when three engineers grew tired of watching great strategy decks collect dust. We set out to build a consultancy that stays in the room through delivery — not just the planning phase.

Today, our team of 40+ strategists, designers, and developers has partnered with startups and established enterprises alike, helping them plan smarter and ship faster without sacrificing quality.
